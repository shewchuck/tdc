Project Overview

Background (not sure if I want to keep this heading)

Stolen from someone's site purely for reference...shameless. The scope of the project was to refresh the existing website, giving it a more contemporary design, increased usability, optimized UX, meeting user expectations while supporting internal business needs (GIVE ME TEH MONEYS) and meeting our user's where they are (mindlessly browsing on their mobile devices). With a mobile-first approach the key focus, the website was re-designed from top to bottom from product cards, navigation, iconography and layout.
