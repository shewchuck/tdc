Work Experience

For the past 25 years, I’ve had the pleasure of working alongside extremely talented creatives and development partners, creating web experiences for amazing clients & companies.

Currently, I’m an Associate Creative Director and Principal Developer alongside the fine folks at One Design Company. In my unique design engineering position, I’m able to exercise both sides of my brain — working as both a web designer as well as a full stack developer. The mix of skills is a great way to see a project through from inception to launch. Previously, I was one of a few key team members at the Noun Project — focusing on product design in an agile environment.
