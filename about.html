Designing clear, effective digital experiences through collaboration, research, and informed decision-making.

Todd Dunn is a digital designer with over two decades of experience working on complex digital products and experiences. He works closely with partners to translate business goals and user needs into clear, effective design solutions—balancing research, data, and informed judgment to support confident design decisions. His approach blends collaboration, pattern recognition, and a strong foundation in UX and UI fundamentals to create experiences that are both practical and thoughtfully crafted.

Experience

Todd’s experience reflects a long-term focus on designing and evolving complex digital products within real business environments.
