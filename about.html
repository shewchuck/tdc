Experience

Approach

My approach is structured but pragmatic. I use research, analytics, and experimentation to inform decisions — adjusting depth and rigor based on the context, risk, and business objectives. I prioritize clarity over density, alignment over urgency, and systems over one-off solutions.

I operate comfortably across strategy and execution, from defining hypotheses and experimentation roadmaps to prototyping, implementation collaboration, and post-launch evaluation.
